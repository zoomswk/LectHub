FAQ

What format of video files do you support?

We support MP4 and MOV files.

Can we override the generated captions?

Yes, you can! Click once and then just edit!

What about OCW or edX?

OCW is not an open platform - the content is regulated by the university. We are very open and would welcome your lectures! edX does not let you interact by editing subtitles and asking questions.

What about Wikipedia?

Wikipedia is text-based, while we are video based and more interactive!

What about Quora?

Quora is only question and answer, but we provide lecture videos from top-notch professors in top universities!

What about Youtube?

Youtube doesn’t let you change subtitles on-the-fly. Plus, we support LaTeX!

What about Reddit?

We plan to have comment threads in each video, just like Reddit! But we are educational, not general - so you can we sucked into a black hole of education!
